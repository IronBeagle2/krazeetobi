MARIO PAINT
December 10th, 1992
DRAWING

The first family-use amusement software for the SNES using the mouse. With the simple operation of the mouse, players can expand the world of Mario Paint to stimulate their creativity. The three main features of the game are drawing pictures, animating them, and composing music using the built-in sound source, but the game has the potential to expand into multiple areas depending on the player's creativity. The works can also be captured on video. Find your own way to enjoy it for games, hobbies, and practical use.
