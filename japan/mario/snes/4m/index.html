Super Mario All-Stars

The dream Mario quadruple feature. The NES Super Mario series; Super Mario Bros., Super Mario Bros. 2, Super Mario Bros. 3, and Lost Levels have been combined
into one and returned to the SNES! Of course, the graphics and sound have been upgraded. The game comes with a backup battery that allows you to save your progress, so you can challenge even the most difficult stages. Another nice bonus of the SNES version is that you can now play Mario Bros., a 2-player game that was popular in Super Mario Bros. 3 from the main menu.
