SUPER MARIO WORLD
April 11th, 1992
ACTION

The NES version of Super Mario Bros., which became a worldwide breakout hit, is now available on the SNES with even more power. The stage is set in Dinosaur Land. Mario or Luigi must defeat the persistent Bowser's army. Of course, there are plenty of new actions and characters to choose from. A new map mode has also been introduced to polish up the fun and excitement. Don't overdo it with Super Mario World, the ultimate technical fantasy game.
