SUPER MARIO KART
January 21st, 1993
RACE

Choose your favorite driver and kart from eight different types, then head to the starting line. 24 unique and mysterious courses await you. You can attack enemy karts by taking items on the course. You can also increase your speed by earning coins. This is a new racing game with lots of action and chances to turn the tables. Take the No. 1 position by any means necessary!
