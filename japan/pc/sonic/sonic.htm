In response to positive feedback on the bundle
Until now, Sonic CD has only been available bundled with PCs, or rather, only from us. In Japan, it was only available to those who purchased Toshiba's Brezza. However, in response to user requests to play it, we are releasing an upgraded version.

Smoother and closer to the original operation
Further development from the bundled Sonic CD, depending on the performance of your computer, will enable an operation feel closer to the original work. For example, it will be possible to accept input 60 times per second, the same as in the original work. With a better PC, you will be able to meet the real Sonic at 60 times per second, which is the closest to the original work.

Opening with a difference
For this development, the quality of the opening animation was improved. In addition, the BGM for the opening and ending animation is the same as that used in the U.S. It would be a shame to ignore it just because you saw it once on the Mega CD! Take another look!

New "Time Warp" element
This time, Sonic can time warp back and forth between the past, present, and future. And without exploring all three time periods, there will never be true peace on Little Planet!

3D Bonus Stage
The promised bonus stage of the Sonic series. This time, the game is in 3D, and it is a game of catching UFOs. (UFO catcher, in other words?) If you think that catching UFOs is easy because they are moving slowly, you are wrong! The floor is full of traps, including a fan that sends Sonic flying through the air. Players will have to see for themselves what happens when they get all seven timestones.

Time Attack Mode
As the name suggests, this mode is to compete for the time to run from the start to the finish line. Aim for a new record. If you shorten the total time...?
