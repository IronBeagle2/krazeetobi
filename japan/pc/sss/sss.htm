Sonic Screen Saver

　　　　　(c)SEGA ENTERPRISES,LTD. 1996

The popular Sonic appears in the screensaver. Illustrations of Sonic appear one after another, and it is naturally Sonic and his friends who act as the exchangers. The screen saver is a software application that prevents the screen from burning out when the computer is not in use, but it is so fun that you can't help but look at it.



Completely newly drawn characters
The four (?) familiar characters who rampage around the screen and show us various actions are Sonic, Tails, Knuckles, and Eggman. The animation patterns for these characters have all been newly drawn for this development. We hope that you will enjoy their new activities, which no one has seen yet. The level of graphics of the characters and the amount of animation patterns are all unprecedented in screensavers.

Abundant illustrations
The illustrations that Sonic and his friends replace have been found and included in calendars, packages, and other materials. Some of these illustrations are from overseas, which are not easily seen in Japan. The number of illustrations in the collection is more than 80!

Equipped with an animation editor!
The animation editor allows the user to set the order in which the animation patterns appear. If you are happy just watching Sonic, you can set it so that only Sonic appears in succession.
