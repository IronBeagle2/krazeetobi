A NiGHTS REMIX
Another Dreams


Released by: Tokuma Japan
Release date: 1997/7/23
Item number: TKCA-71192
Price: 2,500 yen (including tax)

Knights remix disc released by Tokuma Japan.
It came with a sticker with the same design as the jacket.
The obituary says "including Christmas Nights material", but I'm referring to the acapella version of "Dreams Dreams" (not the full chorus) that was included in the power play.
At the same time, a club remix based on a racing game called "Psyched" was also released.
I'd like to introduce some comments from Hatani-san that I received.

Like many remix albums, this album, as the title suggests, is distinct from the NiGHTS worldview, and is more of a slice of NiGHTS' music that was cut up and made to function in the club, perhaps a "dance" album rather than a "listen" album.
It is something to listen to while dancing at high volume along with the collaged images of NiGHTS.
Those who have enjoyed the world of NiGHTS will enjoy it with a sense of wonder.
We would like to pursue such secondary post-game pleasures more and more in the future.
This is one piece that gives such a premonition. (Hatani)
