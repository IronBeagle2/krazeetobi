A tribute disc of video game music celebrating the 20th anniversary of the appearance of Space Invaders.
Basically, guitarists are cast as the main players.
The album closes with "Dreams Dreams", a quieter version with an acoustic guitar.
It's like, no matter how you listen to it, it's an ovation.
