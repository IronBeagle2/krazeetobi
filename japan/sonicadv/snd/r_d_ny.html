 All about New York Recording? is now available to the public!
From July 15 to 29, 1998, I went to New York City for recording.
I was quite prepared to go there because it was in New York, the world's largest city and a racial salad bowl, but the people and sounds I encountered there were truly warm and welcoming.
Please take a look at our story.
I divided the data into a diary and photos because the file would become too large if I included photos.
