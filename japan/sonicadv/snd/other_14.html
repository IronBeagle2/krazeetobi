BURNING RANGERS


Released by: Marvelous Entertainment Inc.
Distributed by: Pony Canyon Inc.
Release date: 1998/2/18
Item number: MJCA-00014
Price: 2,310 yen (including tax)

Soundtrack disc of Burning Ranger, a project led by Oshima-san of Sonic Team.
The sound staff was led by Captain Hatani, who was also joined by Kumatani.
Come to think of it, the only neat projects she's done since she joined the company have been with Sonic Team....
I got her comment on that.
This was the second time I experienced my song being made into a CD.
Also, it's a sonic team.
This is a work that has started to have a little bit of leeway and stretch out. (Kumatani)
I also got a message from Captain Hatani.
The sound planning for Burning Ranger began with sound effects, and we wondered if we would have enough songs for a soundtrack, but as it turned out, we were able to create a soundtrack that traces the game scene step by step, with several songs that enhance the event scenes.
This soundtrack also includes the English version of the theme song used in the international version.
Please compare the Japanese and English versions, as each has a different flavor.
The theme song was recorded by Kosugi-san of "Beat on Beat," who has also been fully involved in Sonic Adventure.
The works you create, Kosugi-san, always have a good 80's sound. (Hataya)
