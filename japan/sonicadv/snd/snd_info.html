Jan./20/1999
 CLUB SEGA 2 on sale!
"CLUB SEGA" was released last year and has established a new movement in the club scene and has been on NTV's "Countdown Groove" show for over 4 months.
As the title suggests, it is a club mix of SEGA's game sounds, and the second in the series, "CLUB SEGA 2 ~Beat Grooves~" has been released.
For this album, I'm taking vocal songs with English lyrics as the subject matter and remixing them by dance music creators from all over the world.
Since we're presenting it here, there's something about the Sonic team... I guess that's what I'm saying.
In fact, songs like this one have been remixed.

Work It Out ...from SONIC R
We Are Burning Rangers ...from BURNING RANGERS
Dreams Dreams ...from NiGHTS
I Just Smile ...from BURNING RANGERS

Jan./8/1999
 MIDI file of Open Your Heart
included in DTM Magazine!
Literally, it's a magazine for people who enjoy DTM (desktop music), and in it is an interview about the sound of Sonic Adventure.
The page it's on is p. 23, not p. 44 as stated in the table of contents... Check it out.
But I still think that the tokoikun in this picture is not quite right....

Also, this magazine has a serialized section called "Game Music Fan Club", where we pick up a song in each issue and create MIDI data for it.
In the past, songs from NiGHTS and Sega Rally 2 have been featured, but this time, Open Your Heart is the subject of this project.
The CD-ROM that comes as an appendix has MIDI data, so check it out if you are interested.
