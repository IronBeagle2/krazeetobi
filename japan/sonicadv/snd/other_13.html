SONIC R


Released by: Marvelous Entertainment Inc.
Distributed by: Pony Canyon Inc.
Release date: 1997/10/17
Item number: MJCA-00012
Price: 2,310 yen (including tax)

The soundtrack disc of Sonic R, which was the second Project Sonic.
The danceable sound, which eventually became almost entirely vocalized, was created by Richard of SEGA of Europe.
The CD's jacket and other design elements were created by Uekawa, who was in charge of character design for Sonic Adventure.
