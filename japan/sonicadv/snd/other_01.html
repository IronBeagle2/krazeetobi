SING!!
SEGA GAME MUSIC
Presented by B.B.Queens


Released by: BMG Victor Corporation
Release date: 1992/7/26
Item number: BVRR-1
Price: 3,000 yen (including tax)

In addition to the game "Teddy Boy Blues" for MEGA-CD, songs from various games are arranged as vocal songs.
Sonic is only depicted on the cover and not used in any way in the song.
As the subtitle suggests, a number of so-called "being" musicians, including B.B. Queens, participated in the festival.
Maki Ohguro sings After Burner and OutRun, for example.
Keiko Utoku, a familiar face from Sonic CD, handles the quiz scramble.
Speaking of quiz scramble, it's the pie-throwing, isn't it!


I want to see the next one!
