The fourth related CD is the original soundtrack of Sonic Adventure!
What does the title "Digi-Log Conversation" mean? See the booklet of the CD!
By the way, as you may know, "Digi-Log" stands for "Digi" in "Digital" and "Log" stands for "Log" in "Analog".
It seems that we wrote and recorded more songs than we expected this time, and although we tried to include just enough of each on the 2 CD set, we couldn't fit them all in. Too bad!

Yes, we have focused on many things in this CD.
I didn't want the booklet to be like a regular game CD, with credits and comments and such.
If you buy a movie soundtrack or something like that, there's no commentary from the creator.
What we are making, of course, is game music.
However, we are confident that we have created something that can walk on its own, even if it is only in terms of music.
That's why we divided the booklet into two parts, one for the music and one for the game.
It's something that expands and gets bigger, and the back can be a poster.
We also designed the CD's disc....
In many ways, we have created a satisfying product.

Nevertheless, once the work on this CD is finished, the work on Sonic Adventure will be over... I remember feeling strangely "sad" rather than "happy" because of this.
