SEGA Vocal Entertainment


Released by: Tokuma Japan
Release date: 1997/8/21
Item number: TKCA-71210
Price: 3,059 yen (including tax)

An attempt to create a "CD with voice actors singing" the vocal songs used in the game.
The sixth song, "Dreams Dreams," is the original version sung by a child.
Incidentally, two of the songs from the bright crystal legend Astal were included as they were, since there was no soundtrack disc released and the voice actors sang them at the time of the original.

01. Let Me Try Again ... Aya Kugawa - The Legend of Astal
02. DREAMS DREAMS ... INOUE Kazuhiko - NiGHTS
 03. Future ... Miuraura - Dragon Force
04. TRY !!! ... Anki Kawamizuru - Victory Goal Worldwide Edition
05. Clockwork Knight ... Takano Rei - Clockwork Knight
06. DREAMS DREAMS (Original Version) ...Jasmine Ann Allen/Cameron Earl Strother - NiGHTS
 07. A lullaby ... Takano Rei - Clockwork Night
08. Shining World ... Aya Kugawa - Shining Crystal Legend Astal
09. Courage ... Madoka Akita - Dragon Force
10. Across Thousands of Nights ... Kazuhiko Inoue - VICTORY GOAL '96
