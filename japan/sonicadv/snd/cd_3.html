SONIC ADVENTURE related
Music CD Vol. 3!

SONIC Adventure REMIX

Released by: Toshiba EMI
Release date: 1998/12/10
Item number: TOCP-65024
Price: 2,548 yen (including tax)

Chris Vrenna (ex-Nine Inch Nails), Kevin Moore (ex-Dream Theater), Chris Randall (Sister Machine Gun), Corey Glover (ex-Living Colour), renowned session guitarist Al Pitrelli and more... A remix album featuring a host of superb artists worthy of Sonic Adventure's Dreamcast debut!

It comes with a sticker of the main visual of SONIC!

The third related CD is a remix album inspired by the vocal songs on the mini-album!
Many remixes are released later, but this was an early release.
The line I aimed for with this CD is like "SPAWN -THE ALBUM-".
I see! I see!

Did you know that some of the commercials for several different games that have been produced have used some of these songs?
The image on the jacket with the visuals from the production presentation fits the image!

But why is there no remix featuring Sonic's theme!?
1. UNKNOWN from M.E. -Theme of "KNUCKLES"-
remixed by Kevin Moore (ex.DREAM THEATER) & Steve Tushar
It makes use of rap music not used on the mini-album.

2. MY SWEET PASSION -Theme of "AMY"-
remixed by Kevin Moore (ex.DREAM THEATER) & Steve Tushar
Voices, you use them well.

A remix that caught me by surprise in a good way.
I really like the way the whispering voice is handled. (Tokoi)
I think I like the flanging over the various parts, the momentary "pause" in the middle of the song, and the rising atmosphere that follows. (Senoue)
3. MY SWEET PASSION -Theme of "AMY"-
remixed by Chris Vrenna (ex.NINE INCH NAILS) & Mark Blasques
featuaring... Corey Glover (Vo) & Al Pitrelli (Gt)

I like this kind of thing.
