01. Introduction
...featuring "Open Your Heart"
Music & Arrangement: J.Senoue & F.Kumatani Lyrics: J.Senoue & T.Fukada
The song that opens this work is the song from the opening movie of the game.
In this movie, the song is played all the time... Instead, we thought from the beginning, "Let's use only the chorus of the main theme!" I had this in mind from the very beginning.
I think the result was impact-oriented, well-paced, and full of tension.
When this song was written, we invited the Sonic Team staff to listen to it in the studio, and at that point, we were already convinced that it was going to be good.
Kumatani did exactly what we wanted in the introduction of this song.
I still get excited when I hear it. (Senoue)
The introduction, I think I did a cool job.
When the image was first communicated to me, I could easily accept it, perhaps because it also overlapped with my own image.
There was some difficulty, but after a little bit of work, we were able to gain momentum and complete the project in one fell swoop.
I am satisfied, very satisfied. (Kumatani)
