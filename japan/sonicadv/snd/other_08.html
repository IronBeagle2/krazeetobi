This was my first experience of having my songs made into a CD.
This is a tense piece of work from my rookie year when I was suddenly assigned to do a project for Sonic Team. (Kumatani)
Speaking of NiGHTS, our general manager said he was going to make a sound effect, but he was too busy with work! He only made "one sound effect" and left it to others, but the story is still talked about.
