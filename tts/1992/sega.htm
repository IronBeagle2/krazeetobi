Sega at Tokyo Toy Show 1992

Sega Mega Drive

Sonic the Hedgehog 2

After being shown at the Summer Consumer Electronic Show this year, Sonic the Hedgehog 2 was shown over in Japan. The game looks brilliant, but sorta feels like the first Sonic all over again with a grassy area with some waterfalls. The game also sounds exactly like the first game, though we're to expect that to be ironed out before release.

Sonic also has a new friend, whose name is Miles "Tails" Prower, though we didn't see him in the demo we played. Say what you will, but I think this may turn out to be an excellent game.
