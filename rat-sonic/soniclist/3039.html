Just a short note to inform you that "No Peace in Our Time" , my sequel to
"As the Wheel Turns" has just been released , and you can either e-mail me

Here's a short description of No Peace .
It occurs almost immediately after the events in AtWT , with Sally thinking
she's to blame for Mobius going to war again ... and guess who's job it is
to persuade her otherwise ? Meanwhile , Mobotropolis is the target for the
first strike of the forces loyal to Ari and his allies , but the attack comes
from the place they least expect ... it would seem that Ari's forces have
their own ... connections .
