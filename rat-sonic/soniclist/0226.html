storyteller! But I'm not giving up on this one. I can't say when exactly

advent of summer school. Ami Pro is getting the workout of its encoded
life (yes, I will save it as a text file). Another thing is it looks as if
it's going
to be a fairly sizable piece of work, extrapolated from what I have in the
file now. A picture paints 1000 words, and I'm trying to take it from the
opposite end. It's been a fun project so far, and I promise it will be com-
pleted.
