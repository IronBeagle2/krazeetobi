FurToonia's Telnet address is 198.102.68.2 8888; the last number is
important, as it's a port number (saves you having to answer the usual
intimidating login prompt). You can call and connect to 'guest' (the
program gives you instructions once you connect); to get a character of
your own, you have to send email to ftadmin_at_rtd.com, including the
character name and password that you want in the message.
