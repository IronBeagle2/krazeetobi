Why don't we make a list for this project. That way, everybody will know what
everybody else is doing at the moment. Plus, the game will turn out a LOT
more organized (Let's hope we don't hit the saving Robuttnik sceme:'). I have
seen these kind of things happen). In fact, why don't we just use this list?
That way, the money will be saved, the info will be sent, and the viewers
will see the update on the game!
