Dear friends

     Hi, I just joined this mailing list. I've bought Issue's #1 and #2
of the "Princess Sally" mini-series, but I haven't been able to find #3
anywhere in Austin. Also, you can only buy all three issues at once through
the mail-order form. So can anyone on this list get a copy of "Princess
Sally #3" for me? I'll pay back for the comic and the shipping, of course.
     BTW: So what did people think about the mini-series ( I read the
synopsis of #3 in the FAQ, so I already know what happened there. )? A big
chunk of issues #1 and #2 were just fight scenes with robots. But I did like
the scheme involving Geoffrey. Much better writing than the "Sonic" issues
had a year ago.
     And I really liked Geoffrey St. John's character design. Sonic, like
most other mainstream male furries, is drawn as a ball with pipe-limbs stuck
in him. But Geoffrey has a physique that's as anthropomorphic as Sally's.
And he's drawn very muscular, but not to the point of caricature, like Arnold
in TTA.

     See ya around

 - Juan F. Lara
