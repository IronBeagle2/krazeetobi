Greetings:

All I know is that Archie Comics honors subscriptions in the US and
Canada -- I've had to enter a subscription in my own name and ship the
issues to a Mobian sympathizer in Mexico.

As for S. Africa -- if Archie doesn't have a rep in the country, it
would cost way more than the US$15 subscription price just to mail
issues to you so that they get there in a timely fashion (at the library
where I work, I'm in charge of forwarding periodicals to a college
library in Nigeria so this is not outside my realm of experience).

I'm wondering if the Fleetway comix get to you down there.
