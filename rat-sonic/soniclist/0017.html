Hello, fellow Mobius Freedom Fighters!

Thank you for the indoctrination to the organization that hopefully
will save the planet from Robotnik. I'm 20 years old, a student at

Sonic shows (excluding Robotnik, Snively, SWATBots, etc.). I've
seen nearly all of the Sonic shows (that's "Sonic the Hedgehog",
not "Adventures of Sonic the Hedgehog"; "ASH" is verrry bad (IM
HO)). I haven't read any of the comics, but I'm aware that there's
at least 2 available: "Sonic the Hedgehog" and "Princess Sally".
I can talk to anyone concerning the shows, and I'll try not to spoil
the ending in advance if you haven't seen it (notify me first).

Chow y'all. Later!
