3) Reply

OK , now for the last part .
The magazine Electronic Gaming Monthly's May issue (we just got it here in
Singapore , and I'm not sure how long ago it was released elsewhere ) had a
half page article on what they call " Super Sonic " , the working title for
the Sonic game on the Saturn . They say the release date is in Fall , it is
action themed , and is 25% complete . The game is in 3D , and will either be
rendered , like the arcade game , or polygon-based like the Sonic Fighting
Game . EGM also said that Sega would not comment further until the E3 trade
show .
