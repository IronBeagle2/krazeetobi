            Sega's coming back to Sonic
                    in Force!!!

An Electronic Gaming Monthly report on the E3 confrence tells of a new
Sonic game coming for the _Genesis_ (I hope I hope!!) I don't know
all the details, but I do know that it'll be called 'Sonic 3-D', is
expected to come out by Christmas, and will have a 3/4 perspective a
la Mario RPG.

I'll be scanning the article in for rat.org and be hanging on to my
16-bitter! I don't think that Sega's done with the Genesis yet!!

Oh, and do you know that Saturns got _three_ 32-bit processors?
That's *96* bits there!! (3-by-32--remember the Turbo Graphix 16?
Really a 2-by-8?) What's Nintendo's mystery system got? A lowly
1-by-64? That processor'll be having strokes by the time I'm done
with it...and it'll *still* play that pond scum plummer!!
