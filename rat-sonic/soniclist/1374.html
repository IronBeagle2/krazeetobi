IT'S FINALLY FINISHED!! :)

Hello, and welcome to my third and final Sonic story, completing a trilogy
(more or less) that started with "Tolouse's Story" and continued with
"Run with the Fox". Sorry it took so long. This has been a very unmotivating
summer. :) A very special thanks to Craig "Todd" Moore, without
whom this story would not have existed in quite the same way. Other
"thank you"s and "hiya"s go to all the FurTooniacs: Sonic, Sally, Tails,
Bookshire, Rotor, MetalFox, Monolith, Mason, Nemo, Nikko, and many many more.
