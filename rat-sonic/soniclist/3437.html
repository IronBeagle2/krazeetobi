When I found out about this list and the other Sonic fandom on the Net
about a...JEEZ-UM, A YEAR AGO?!?!?! How time does fly...anywayz, as I was
saying, I was in heaven when I found this territory, and I'm beyond sorry
to see it go. This has been a true meeting place, where I can converse
with those who share my interests...it's like everything I wanted Sonic
'94/'95/'96 to be, but, for various complicated reasons, it didn't turn out
as such (for one thing, it's INCREDIBLY off-topic, which is why Sir Kain
left Prodigy). It's been great, guys. <Bows low> At the risk of sounding
like a hypocrite: Try not to be TOO bitter about this, guys, hard though it
may be. The newsgroup remains, and there will be more mailing
lists...because Sonic the Hedgehog will NEVER DIE.
