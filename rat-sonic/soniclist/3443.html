Well ... it seems like this is goodbye .

I've only been on this list for two months , but I think I've met many
interesting people via this list in that short space of time .

It's kept me up-to-date on a whole Sonic world I never knew existed before
joining this list . It inspired me to start writing my own fan fictions AND
start a webpage devoted to the blue spiky one .

Thanks to all who have spent time and resources on maintaining this list .

Let me take this opportunity to make some final announcements :

1)I'm really disheartened that I have not recieved any fan fiction contributions
within the last month or so ... it looks like my Archives are a failure . :(
I will wait for the day that another fanfic graces my screen , but I think I
might be waiting in vain ...

If no fanfics come , then I will sadly terminate my Sonic Webpage and devote
the resources to other topics .

2)This is probably one of the last fan fictions I will write . It is called
Alterations and will wrap up my Foxes and Sorcery series . I *may* write one
more to conclude the Wheel series , but no confirmation as yet .

Anyone interested in "Alterations" can e-mail me directly at
prower_at_pacific.net.sg for a copy of it .

3)I'm really sad that this list has to go ...

To conclude , I guess all that's left to say is farewell ....
