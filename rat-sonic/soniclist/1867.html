Sounds like you were a little confused by the "Doomsday" ep. You've got
company: the jump-cutting during the finale didn't help matters at all.

As to how Sally got that way, you have to go back to the "Drood Henge"
ep. In that case, the plot revolved around something called the Deep
Power Stones. Joined in one configuration they were supposed to be a
source of limitless power; joined in another configuration they were
able to destroy...I don't know...the whole planet?

I humbly suggest you check the FAQ File, which summarizes the episodes
in roughly the order in which they were first aired.
