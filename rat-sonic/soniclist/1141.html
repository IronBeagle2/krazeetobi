These are books; novellas is more like it. Each is well under 100
pages, with occasional full-page illustrations by Glen Hanson.
Teitelbaum's style has improved since the first book; he's gotten the
hang of writing in Sonic's hip argot. One other notable feature: he
plays Antoine extremely straight -- no jive Clouseau accent or general
bumbling. You can find the books in the Juvenile Fiction center of your
local bookstore, with the latest Goosebumps volume and the novelization
of the MMPR movie (ack!).
