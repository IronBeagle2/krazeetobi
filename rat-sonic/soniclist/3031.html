Probably not, or else the force of gravity would be much less. Based on the
way we've seen Sonic and all of the other freedom fighters move, the
gravitational field of Mobius is probably pretty much identical to that of
our own. However, it's possible that Mobius has some unique properties to it
that we don't understand. Maybe there's something different about the
gravity of the Mobian sun, as well as its surrounding planets, that could
compensate for a smaller planetary mass in terms of the gravity field.
