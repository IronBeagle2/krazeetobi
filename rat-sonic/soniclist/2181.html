To round things out (while getting off-topic):

Kath Soucie also does double-voicing as the twins Phil and Lil on
Nickelodeon's "Rugrats" [where, BTW, Christine Cavanaugh is the voice of
Chuckie]. Kath has also done some voice work for Disney: she's done two
Aladdin eps that I know of: she was the title role in "The Entity" and
she voiced a poor girl on the classic Aladdin ep "Do The Rat Thing"
where she spoke that timeless line: "It's the Magic Holiday Rat! She
brought us a present from the Rodent King!"
