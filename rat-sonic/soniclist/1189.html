I have just finished adding Mobius to TV Net (http://tvnet.com/TVnet.html).

I've included this list, both FAQ's, the episode list, and two newsgroups
(a.f.s-h and r.a.a). For compatibility with TV Net's FAQ retrieval, I
changed the ftp site storage of faq-dd from 3 parts back to 1 part.
(If you use ftpmail, the server should be able to break up the file into
64K chunks, if you cannot receive messages >64K.)

In addition to TV Net, we are listed in the Publicly Accessible Mailing
Lists (PAML) list and the rec.arts.tv FAQ. We might also be in AOL's
private list of mailing lists, but I can't check that because I'm not on AOL.
I also know of 3 Web pages with links to the list's page
(http://www.hff.rat.org/sonic).
