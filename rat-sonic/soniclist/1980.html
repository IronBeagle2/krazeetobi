I have another game suggestion:

Ever since I saw and played my first Sonic game, I wished that someone would
make a game where Sonic doesn't have a speed limit. Maybe in this game, when
Sonic uses a power ring, the speed limit could be the power limit of the
computer. You could use something like press and hold forward to accelerate,
let go to slow down, and press back to hit the breaks. I know that that might
sound more like a car racing game, but then, I would really like to see Sonic
break the speed (and videogame :') ) barrier on a home computer screen!
