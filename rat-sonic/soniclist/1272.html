Lately this thread has been strectching the limits of "on-topic"-ness
with some messages beginning to get into game hints and others having
little to do with Sonic.

The music on the Sonic CD is "on-topic" because:
  - the CD is merchandise
  - the music enhances the story behind Sonic and the other Freedom Fighters
It's also OK to discuss the lyrics and to compare/contrast lyrics and
melodies of these songs with other music that may or may not be on the CD.

I can let these threads go by occasionally because the mailing list is
not too big and tends to keep a pretty good signal to noise ratio. But
we still need to remember that this is the Sonic list whose primary focus
should be on Mobius.

Enough with the lesson. Let's go back to Knothole.
