UK OK COMPETITION!





Been on any top days out recently?
Or was your last family outing a real washout?!
Tell us about it on the UK OK competition!

UK OK is a brand new series on CBBC next year.
It's all about having a great time out in the UK.

We want to hear from YOU about YOUR
MOST UNFORGETTABLE DAY OUT!

It could be your best day out or your worst day out...
At the greatest theme park or the tackiest funfair,
the weirdest museum or the zaniest zoo.
Funny, Fantastic, Dreary or Disastrous - we want to know!

The only rule - it has to be in the UK. OK?!!

A panel of judges will read your entries and choose 3 winners
in each category.
a)under 7 yrs b)8-10 yrs c)11-15 yrs


WHAT YOU COULD WIN!

1st prize winners get a top camera.
2nd prize winners get a family trip to an attraction of your choice
(maximum value £75)
3rd prize winners get a UK OK goody rucksack.

And of course the winning reports will feature on the programme.


WHAT YOU HAVE TO DO...

Just write about your outing in no more than 200 words.
Tell us where it was and why it was UNFORGETTABLE -
what made it exciting, or what went wrong!

If you like you can send pictures or photos
but we will not be able to send them back.


So remember -
write your name,
your age,
your address,
and telephone number
on your entry
