The

RED NOSE

Zone

 

The Friday Zone has now finished its first series. But what a great show to end on, an action packed Red Nose Zone!

 

Peter Simon got his own back on Chris Jarvis with the help of Bodger & Badger. Instead of Peter getting covered in custard, gravy and water, Chris got splattered with mashed potato!

Lots of special guests visited the studio during the show,

Dominic Bel-Geddes and Daniel Cake-Bread from Live & Kicking decided that the Set-the-Set area needed some of their talented action painting, which meant throwing buckets of paint all over the walls, and Peter Simon!!

Mark Speight from SMart helped out with Set-the-Set, but managed to get more paint on himself than on the walls.

Zoë Ball also tried her hand at painting, she wrote "Hello Mum" across the Set-the-Set wall!

Debra showed off to everybody with her backward talking. We recorded it, and played it backwards and we could hear her saying, "It's your show so its your go!"

Otis the Ardvark dropped by to get sponsors for a sponsored silence. Debra was surprised to find out that Otis was not on a sponsored silence, but Richard from CBBC was!

Dominic was disappointed to find that his red nose was very different to everyone elses.

In Lookalike larks Michaela Strachan, the Anorak, and Mel B and C from the Spice Girls, gave Chris Jarvis a headache when he tried to work out who was the real celebrity.

Josie, Debra and Emma tried to be the Space Girls! It all got a bit too much when Steve and Dominic joined in with kettle drums. Chris had the last laugh when he managed to visit the real Spice Girls in their dressing room!

Sage and Onion, the Leprechauns from Live & Kicking, went collecting for Comic Relief. Onion made a mistake on a sign that read "Please give MONKEY to Comic Relief." So all they ended up with was a monkey!

The "Nozone" showed Chris Jarvis and the rest of the Friday Zone presenter's new single for Comic Relief. The "Glasses" C.D. is in the shops now and all profits raised go to Comic Relief. It even has a Karaoke version that you can sing along with!

Jokes from the Red Nose Zone's Joke Box Jury.
