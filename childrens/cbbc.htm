STANDBY FOR STUDIO 9!


There's top news from Children's BBC because Otis, Kirsten, Richard and Dominic are moving to a brand new Children's BBC studio!

Our new home is called Studio 9 and it's totally cool. There's a whole new set to discover, a big new video wall shaped like a number 9 and even monster plants Otis will have to remember to feed. (I wonder if they like toffees and ants, like him?!)
