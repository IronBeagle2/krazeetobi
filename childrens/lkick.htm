Well, are you missing us yet? It's only been a few weeks since we finished the series and we thought we would remind you of some of the highlights.

We were part of the Spice Girls invasion from the beginning and the girls have been on the show 3 times but the highlight had to be when our Jamie asked Geri Spice out. She said no and as far as we know things haven't changed. Other guests who made multiple appearances on the show are Boyzone (4 times), Ant and Dec and 911 (3 times) and Paul Nicholls who came on twice! We are just so popular, they couldn't stay away. But you must have had your favourite so why not let us know who you liked and maybe we could get them on next series!

The studio was full to bursting every week and the Hot Seat proved a bit of a challenge to some! Lennox Lewis, Heavyweight Champion of the World was floored when asked if he was going to settle down and have children, Betty Boothroyd, Speaker of the House of Commons and Paul Burger, Chairman of Sony Music both agreed that they would like to see more women in business, Sean Blowers came in to tell us about his Round The World yacht trip and rang in to chat to mates Robson and Jerome a few weeks later as he neared Australia; and Alan Shearer wants to get his Geordie accent back!!

People do some strange things when they come on to Live & Kicking! egged on by resident funsters, Trev and Simon and The Leprechauns. Sting and Jimmy Nail became art critics, the Sweet Valley High twins ended up dressed up as Scottish Highlanders and Jamie and Zöe even got dressed up as bunnies. And all those video Grand Prix's!!!!! But who could forget Boyzone dressed up as the Spice Girls for our wannabes slot!!

Cloud Nine was a huge success with dreams coming true for loads of viewers, with a little help from The Royal Ballet, Ryan Giggs, a group of Butlins Red Coats and a monkey rescue centre, among others. If you have a dream, get your letters in now for the next series.

And when does the new series start? Well, get your diaries and calenders ready or programme your electronic organiser with this date. Live & Kicking! will be back on the air on

Saturday 27th September.

We can't wait to be back on-line and get all your pictures and letters flooding in. And have a great summer!!!
