Welcome to Wham Bam Strawberry Jam! - the rhyme, rhythm and lots of laughs show.

Rik Mayall stars in the series - - he's performing lots of well-known poems like Roald Dahl's "Cinderella" and A.A. Milne's "The King's Breakfast".

Poets from Scotland, Manchester, Shropshire, Yorkshire and London will join children from each area, to find out about Yuk Love, School Secrets, Trees that Don't Go to School, Heroines and Heroes and Friendship.

Many of the poems performed in Wham Bam Strawberry Jam! have been written specially for this series. If you want to check out information on published poems used in a show, use our Ceefax Page 523.


PROGRAMME 3 : 16th January 1997

15.55 on CBBC 1

Brill!


Rik Mayall performs Gordon Snell's "The King of Quizzical Island" which is all about a King setting off to find the edge of the world and discovering he's returned to his own back door.

Meanwhile poet Lemn Sissay appears as a magical character called "Whoamiiiiii" who only becomes visible when his name is called three times.

He pops up to help a group of children from Manchester work out just exactly who they are - which happens to be, just like you - brill!

The programme was filmed in a fantastic maze at Chatsworth House, in Derbyshire. The Gardens are open every day from the 19th March to the 2nd November, from 11.00 to 17.00 and there's an entrance fee.


PROGRAMME 4: 23rd January

15.55 on CBBC 1

School Secrets


Rik Mayall performs Roger McGough's "Noah's Ark" - an up to date version of the story of Noah, and the animals which go into the ark two by two - along with Mrs. Noah and the rest of the family.

Paul Cookson and David Harmer who are better known as "Spill the Beans", do just that and let us in on all the secrets of life at school. There's the disgusting untidiness of the staffroom and the revolting habits of some teachers, but the pupils also have secrets of their own to be revealed!
