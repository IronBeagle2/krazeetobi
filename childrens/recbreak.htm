Top facts about our presenters:

Kriss Akabusi

Kriss is famous for his achievements in Athletics where his greatest individual triumph was his Gold Medal in the 1990 European Championships when he beat a record which had been unbroken for 22 years. His International career reached a peak in 1983 when he helped Britain take the Gold and beat the Americans in the World Championships 4x400 metre relay in Tokyo.

He broke into television presenting when he stole the show on the Big Breakfast in 1993 and he hasn't stopped working since!


Cheryl Baker

Cheryl's popularity grew after she shot to fame with Eurovision Song Contest winners 'Buck's Fizz'. They went on to have over 20 top selling singles, including 3 number ones.

In 1993 Cheryl left the band to concentrate on her solo career and since then she has made a name for herself as a top television presenter and is now back for her 11th series of Record Breakers

Cheryl is also a fantastic cook and as a result has presented many cookery programmes. She still finds time to pursue her love of cooking but likes nothing better than to be at home with her husband, Steve, and twin girls Kyla and Natalie


Ron Reagan Jnr.

Ron is a native Californian who trained to become a ballet dancer, joining the Joffrey II Dancers in 1980 and toured with the Joffrey Ballet Company.

While touring, he pursued an interest social commentary, writing for many major American magazines. His satirical syle caught the attention of ABC Entertainment who signed him as an on-air reporter. He interviewed many diverse public figures on 'Good Morning America', before hosting his own chat show.


Dan Roland

Dan is Record Breaker’s newest recruit and he join the rest of the team straight from MTV as he has just completed filming a new travel series for MTV Europe. Previous to this he was working for Channel One as a mobile one man reporter / presenter / cameraman, producing rapid turnaround event-based features for London Cable. He spent six months reporting for Paint It Red, Channel One’s daily live entertainment show and four months on Colour Supplement, a lively afternoon magazine slot for higher profile VJ’s. Dan has also presented and produced four intelligent entertainment series, Dan Roland’s Christmas Annual, Dan & Dan’s Fun In The Sun, Eurodan and Dan & Dan’s Christmas Cracker.
