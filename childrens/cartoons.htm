ANTS IN YOUR PANTS

CBBC's star presenter is back! That's right, Otis the Aardvark shows off his artistic talents, squiggles pictures on his easel, eats ants - and of course presents Ants In Your Pants. Every Wednesday on BBC1 Otis invites you to share in the fun, as he presents the brand new series of your favourite cartoons - Oakie Doke, William's Wish Wellingtons and The Little Polar Bear.

Wednesday, 3.30pm, BBC1


Oakie Doke

Oakie Doke is the Mr Fix-it of Oakie Hollows. He lives in a very tall tree on the outskirts of a large, dark pine forest - and he's the guy everyone calls on when they have a problem to solve. Whether it's the energetic Mouse Family, the green-fingered Hedgehogs, the one-parent Frog Family or the mining Mole Family - they all go to Oakie Doke. Oakie Doke may be a bit of a scatterbrain, but he's always good humoured - and he can always help.

William's Wish Wellingtons

Six-year-old William lives with his Mum and Dad, his dog called Barksure - and a secret. Because William has a pair of magical red wellingtons. All he has to do is put them on, and anything he wishes will come true. William's Wish Wellingtons lead him into all sorts of adventures with all sorts of creatures, including dinosaurs, camels and genies.

The Little Polar Bear

Lars the little polar bear is back with his friends Lena the white hare and Piers the snow-goose chick. When curiosity gets the better of him, Lars and his friends end up in all kinds of scrapes and adventures. Lars even falls in love! And the three of them find hidden treasure and team up with a brown bear on the edge of the ice flow.

THE ANIMALS OF FARTHING WOOD

The creatures who inhabit Farthing Wood are in real trouble. Not content with building on Farthing Heath over the past five years, the humans are moving inwards on the wood itself. The bulldozers have already filled in Farthing Wood Pond, and the recent drought has turned local streams into muddy trickles. The animals of Farthing Wood have no water, and if something isn't done soon they will perish.

Badger and Fox, Toad and Kestrel, Owl and Mouse and all the other characters set off on a treacherous journey to find water and somewhere safe to live.

EEK! THE CAT

THE MASK

The Mask brings you action! heroics! and wise-cracking comedy! When nice guy Stanley Ipkiss dons a powerful ancient mask, he transforms into a superhero with loony toon powers. The mask unleashes a person's inhibitions and hidden desires, coaxing the wearer to act upon them.

Stanley's desire to stand up for himself makes for a hilarious though sometimes reluctant superhero, who battles with an array of new super-monstrous villains who constantly threaten the citizens of Edge City.

OSCAR'S ORCHESTRA

The year is seven hundred and thirty-five squillian, twenty-two million, three hundred and eighty-one thousand, six hundred and four! Thadius Vent, world leader and evil dictator has banned music from the world. Instruments that once graced the most beautiful concert halls are now reduced to scraping a living as door bells, fire alarms, car hooters and worse.

Oscar, a bright blue Grand piano, has decided to fight back. He gathers other like-minded instruments together and forms a secret band dedicated to bringing music and happiness back into the world. Travelling through time, space and people's living rooms, Oscar and his friends rediscover their past, their music and their will to play as they battle against the evil Thaddius.

Tuesday, 4.10pm, BBC1


THE REAL ADVENTURES OF JOHNNY QUEST

Johnny Quest may look like an ordinary 13-year-old boy - but he has some extraordinary global adventures in Questworld, his gateway to adventure and fantasy in Virtual Reality, where he and his team can transform into anything they want to be.

Johnny and his team get caught up in adventures in the Amazonian rain forest, the Himalyas, the Kalahari, the Gobi, the Everglades, the ocean's depths, and the Northern and Southern-most extremities of the world.

Surf the Globe with Johnny Quest!!!

ROMUALD

Romuald the Reindeer is a youngster trying to stand on his own four feet. He is always trying to catch Santa's eye in the hope of being one of the chosen few - the reindeer elite who pull Santa's sleigh on Christmas Eve. Unfortunately his attempts to impress Santa always go wrong. Romuald is the sort of reindeer who always throws himself into the deep end and then remembers he can't swim. Romuald rushes in where angels fear to tread!
