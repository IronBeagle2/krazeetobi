SMart returns in the autumn.

The next series of the fun art programme SMart will be returning to Children's BBC in September. Mark Speight, Jay Burridge and Josie d'Arby will be back together again with more great ideas and things for you to make using simple materials, and showing original ideas to help with your artwork. And each week there will be a Fact Sheet giving more information about the items.

They would also like to hear from you because each week there are ways for you and your art to be included in the programme.

The Gallery.
Each week we show a selection of artwork sent to the programme by viewers, aged up to 15 years. They can be paintings or drawn pictures, cut-outs or collages, 3-D items or computer graphics - as long as they are your own, original work.

SMart Tips.
If you are having trouble with your art - want to know how to draw eyes, or give a convincing shadow effect - then ask our Agony Artists! Each week they hold their SMaster Class on the programme, and it could be your question they answer.

Competitions.
Throughout the series there will be competitions with fabulous prizes, to give another chance for you to use your design and art skills.
